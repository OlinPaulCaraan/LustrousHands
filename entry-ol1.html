To The Man I Loved

To the man I loved,

Hi, How are you? Do you still remember me? How about the times we had, does it cross over your mind?

Frankly, I still can't forget the moment we had in high school. When we finished performing the speech choir I had to run to the bathroom to wash my feet and use my hanky to wipe off the dirt and suddenly use it to cover your eyes. I know it's gross but we used to be friends and I'm shameless when I do those things especially that I am comfortable around you. You're tall and I'm the opposite, I had to tiptoe to reach your eyes and cover it from the back. Do you know what's the best thing is? that's when you turn around and I lost my balance but you catch me before I fall, your strong arms keeping me away from the ground, holding me close to you, our eyes meet, the world seems in slow motion and the unexpected spark that I felt inside. After that, I always wanted to go early in class excited to see you, because being with you makes me happy.

Do you also remember after school we walk with our friend, and you stop beside me just to put your red headset on me to listen to one direction song, unfortunately, I don't remember what song it is but I felt my heartbeat increase, my hand shakes and I startled, those mixed feelings were all because of you.

After all these 7 years I still feel the love I have for you. I still have this notebook full of words I wanna say and feelings that I kept inside, even the sketch of you and me. I was afraid to tell you this at first but I got the chance. Sadly, you said that we can't be more than friends, you have reason and I have mine. I have no reason to make boundaries just to love you but your point is we both are in a different religion. I'm disappointed because I know that love conquers all, nothing can stop a person to love the other. Those words that came out of your mouth felt like a gushing cold wind down to my heart. I had this feeling that someday I could reach the star that I've been following through night and day. I wanted to fight for the love that I have but you pushed me away. I hesitantly try to forget you and this feeling cause I don't wanna lose you but then I realized that I have to love myself first so that I can find a man who could love me The way I am.

I hope you find a better one that loves you more than I could. Thank you for the memories, I will cherish it with all my heart. Again, thank you and goodbye.

- Angeline
